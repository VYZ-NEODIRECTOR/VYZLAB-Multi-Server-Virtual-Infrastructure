## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Resilience Strategy

The DHCP service is configured in a **Load Balance** mode (50/50) between the two Domain Controllers. This ensures that:

- **High Availability:** If `VYZLAB-DC-01` goes offline, `VYZLAB-DC-02` continues to lease and renew IP addresses for clients.
- **Client Continuity:** Workstations on the `LAN 1` segment experience zero downtime during server maintenance or unexpected failure.
- **Conflict Prevention:** The failover partnership ensures that IP addresses are not duplicated across the subnet.
